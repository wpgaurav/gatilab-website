## Boilerplates

**Short (50 words):**

Gatilab is a digital marketing agency focused on conversion and quality. Founded in 2014, the agency has served 700+ clients globally including IBM, Adobe, HubSpot, and the Government of India. Gatilab specializes in WordPress development, SEO, content marketing, and brand design.

**Long (150 words):**

Gatilab is an independent digital marketing agency founded in 2014 by Gaurav Tiwari and Ravi Shahi. The agency specializes in conversion-focused marketing, delivering high-performance websites, SEO strategies, content marketing, and brand design for businesses ranging from startups to global enterprises.

Over the past decade, Gatilab has served 700+ clients including IBM, Adobe, HubSpot, Canva, Airtel, and the Government of India. The agency has achieved 104% year-over-year growth while driving 330% combined revenue growth for its client portfolio.

What distinguishes Gatilab is its obsession with measurable outcomes. Rather than vanity metrics, the agency measures success through conversions, revenue impact, and business growth. This approach has earned the trust of enterprise clients, government agencies, and growing businesses across 20+ countries.
