## Collecting New Testimonials

### When to Ask
- After successful project delivery
- When client expresses satisfaction
- At milestone achievements
- During annual relationship reviews

### What to Ask
1. What specific problem did we solve for you?
2. What results have you seen since working with us?
3. What was it like working with our team?
4. Would you recommend us? Why?

### Format Template
```
Name: [Full Name]
Title: [Job Title]
Company: [Company Name]
Project: [Project Type]
Quote: "[Their words]"
Results: [Specific outcomes if available]
Permission: [Yes/No for name, company, photo]
Date: [When testimonial was given]
```

### Follow-up
- Send thank-you note after receiving testimonial
- Share where testimonial will be used
- Offer to reciprocate with LinkedIn recommendation

## Reference Policy

### When Prospects Ask for References
1. Confirm what they want to learn (industry fit, capability, reliability)
2. Select 2-3 appropriate references based on their needs
3. Contact references to confirm availability
4. Make introduction via email
5. Follow up with prospect after call

### Reference Prep for Clients
- Give clients heads-up before prospect contact
- Remind them of project details and outcomes
- Thank them for their time afterward
